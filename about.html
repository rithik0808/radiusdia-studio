Leadership

Meet Rithik

Founder & Principal Architect of Radiusdia Studio

With a passion for minimalist aesthetics and deeply functional design, Rithik founded Radiusdia Studio to push the boundaries of modern architecture. Under his vision, the studio has grown into a premier team of designers creating transformative spaces across India.

"Our goal is not just to build structures, but to craft environments that evoke emotion and stand the test of time."
