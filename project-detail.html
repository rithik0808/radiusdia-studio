The Horizon House

Client

Private

Location

Karur, India

Year

2023

Seamless integration of structure and nature.

The Horizon House explores the delicate balance between robust architectural form and the surrounding natural landscape. Situated on a sloped terrain, the residence utilizes massive concrete walls to anchor the structure, while extensive use of glass dissolves the barrier between interior and exterior spaces.

Materiality played a crucial role in the design. Warm-toned wood siding juxtaposes the cool, board-formed concrete, creating a tactile experience that evolves with the sun's passage throughout the day. The minimalist approach eliminates visual clutter, allowing the architecture itself to become a serene backdrop for the clients' lifestyle.
